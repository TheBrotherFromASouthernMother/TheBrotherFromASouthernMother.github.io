Germany is a beautiful country, and nothing enshrines its beauty like its drink culture. The Spring festival is a week long event seen throughout Southern Germany that highlights the regions traditions and ability to party hard. Comparable to Oktoberfest a tourist who finds themselves at the Spring Festival will see young people dressed in lederhosen, beer tents filled to the brim with merry Germans, and vendors selling anything that can be called traditionally German.
